Creating Your Dream Outdoor Space

Owner David England and his staff are dedicated to creating beautiful custom spaces across the region. Dave started Creekside Outdoor as a simple Lawncare service, inheriting the name from his Mother's floral arrangement business. As more and more neighbors sought his keen eye and work ethic, their requests became more and more varied. Today Creekside Outdoor still offers top quality lawncare, but has expanded it's services to include Landscaping, Hardscaping, Outdoor Carpentry, and lawn rehabilitation. If you would like to make the most out of your space, email us or call Dave at (765)-327-5146 today!

Add beauty to your lawn with Landscaping

Adding areas of well designed landscaping is the best way to accentuate the natural beauty of your lawn. We will work with you to design landscaped features that are a perfect fit for your lawn and personal style. Between border styles, fill types, and greenery options the possibilities are endless.
